# Message templates

The bot weaves these into drafts when the situation matches — keep them short
and in your voice. Replace every <PLACEHOLDER>.

## Pickup instructions (home base)

Hi <guest first name>! The car is parked at <PICKUP LOCATION DESCRIPTION>.
<HOW TO FIND THE KEY>. Text us when you're on your way. — <YOUR NAME>

## Drop-off instructions

Just return the car to <RETURN LOCATION>, leave the key <KEY LOCATION>, and
please bring the fuel/charge back to the pickup level. Text us when you've
left the car. — <YOUR NAME>

## Airport / offsite lot pickup (if applicable)

Describe the exact shuttle/lot flow here. The bot applies location gating: it
will only use airport instructions when the trip's real pickup location is the
airport — keep home-base and airport instructions in separate files (see
04-locations.md) so they can't be mixed up.
